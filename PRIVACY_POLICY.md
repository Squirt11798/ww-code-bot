# Privacy Policy — ww-code-bot

**Effective date: June 4, 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains how **ww-code-bot** (the "Bot") handles information.
The Bot is an unofficial, fan-made Discord bot that posts publicly available
Wuthering Waves redemption codes. By using the Bot, you agree to this Policy. The
Bot is in **active development**, and this Policy may change at any time.

## 1. Summary

The Bot is designed to collect **as little information as possible**. It does not
require you to create an account, and it does not ask for, collect, or store your
personal information. The operator of the Bot **takes no responsibility for any
use of the Bot** or for any data processed by third parties (such as Discord) in
connection with it.

## 2. Information the Bot Stores

The only data the Bot persists is a local list of **redemption codes** it has
already posted (so it does not post the same code twice). This data contains **no
personal information** — only public game codes, their reward descriptions, and
the names of the public websites they were found on.

## 3. Information the Bot Does *Not* Collect

The Bot does **not** collect, store, sell, or share:

- your Discord username, user ID, or profile information;
- your messages or message history;
- your email address, IP address, or location;
- payment information (the Bot is free and processes no payments);
- any other personal or identifying information.

The Bot operates without the "Message Content," "Server Members," or "Presence"
privileged gateway intents. It only sends messages and responds to slash
commands.

## 4. Information Processed by Discord

When you interact with the Bot (for example, by using a slash command), Discord
transmits the interaction to the Bot so it can respond. This is handled by
Discord's platform and is governed by **Discord's own Privacy Policy**
(<https://discord.com/privacy>). The operator of the Bot does not control,
retain, or take responsibility for any data handled by Discord.

## 5. Third-Party Websites

The Bot reads publicly available code-listing pages from third-party community
websites. Those sites have their own privacy practices, which the operator of the
Bot does not control and takes no responsibility for.

disclaimer of liability.

## 7. Children's Privacy

The Bot is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the minimum age
required in your jurisdiction). The Bot does not knowingly collect any personal
